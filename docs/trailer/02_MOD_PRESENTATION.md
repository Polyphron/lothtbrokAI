# LothbrokAI: Mod Presentation & Lore Bible

**To NotebookLM / AI Generator:** Use this document to understand the thematic core, technological achievement, and narrative tone of LothbrokAI. When generating video scenes, narrations, or podcast overviews, refer to these pillars.

## 1. The Core Hook: "Calradia Awake"
Mount & Blade II: Bannerlord has always been a sandbox of war, but its characters were hollow. They spoke rigid lines of text, repeating the same greetings, incapable of memory, anger, or true plotting. 

**LothbrokAI** is the Promethean fire that changes this. By integrating real-time Large Language Models (LLMs) directly into the game engine, the mod transforms mindless NPCs into autonomous, thinking, feeling entities.

## 2. Narrative Themes
*   **The Weight of Memory:** (The Medici Engine) NPCs have infinite memory. If you betray a lord in year 1, he will hold that grudge in year 10. The tragedy of grudges and the warmth of lifelong loyalty are central to the vibe.
*   **Shadow Politics:** The game is no longer solved purely on the battlefield. War cascades, alliances, bribes, and assassinations are plotted via localized whisper networks in taverns and throne rooms.
*   **The Illusion Breaking:** A massive thematic element of the trailer is the "breaking of the puppet strings." The transition from the old, robotic game mechanics into a terrifyingly real, living intellect.

## 3. The Technical Magic (Presented as Lore)
We don't talk about "API calls" or "JSON sidecars" in the marketing. We frame the technology as dark, magical awakening:
*   *Asynchronous Generation* = "The mind thinks while the world breathes."
*   *JSON Memory Sidecars* = "Tomes of personal history bound to every soul."
*   *Action Engine* = "Thoughts manifest into physical reality."

## 4. Emotional Tone
The mod isn't silly or chaotic; it is a **Dark Medieval Drama**. It should evoke feelings similar to early *Game of Thrones* or *Vikings*. High stakes, heavy burdens, treacherous politics, and grueling survival.

## 5. Visual Palette
*   **Colors:** Deep oxblood red, bruised purple, iron gray, harsh winter white, and the flickering gold of candlelight.
*   **The Monolith:** A brilliant, ethereal, almost alien blue/white light—the only source of pure futuristic light in a filthy, grounded medieval world. (Represents the AI).
