# LothbrokAI — Cinematic Trailer Storyboard

> **Runtime target:** 2:20 – 2:40
> **Art style:** 2.5D parallax, hand-drawn medieval manuscript illustration. Scratchy black iron-gall ink on aged parchment. Heavy cross-hatching. Candlelight volumetrics. Static illustrative layers floating in 3D depth.
> **Palette:** Sepia, charcoal, dried-blood oxblood, iron gray, harsh winter white, flickering gold candlelight. The Monolith is the ONLY source of blue/white light.
> **Tone:** Dark medieval drama. Early Game of Thrones. Vikings. Heavy, slow, atmospheric.

---

### SCENE 1 — The Frozen Map
**Duration:** 0:00 – 0:18 (18s)
**Soundtrack cue:** Wind and drone only. No percussion. Desolate.

**VISUAL:**
The ink lines of the Calradic map sketch themselves onto rough parchment — but the drawing is rigid, mechanical. Small wooden carved figures of lords are scattered across the map, completely motionless. Dust settles heavily on them. The camera dollies slowly forward over the static pieces. Long candlelight shadows crawl across the frozen map.

**CAMERA:** Slow overhead dolly, drifting south across the map. Slight parallax between map layer and figure layer. 2-3mm of depth.

**AUDIO:** Silence. A quill scratching. Cold winter wind through the bowed drone.

**NARRATION (V.O.):**
*"For an age, the Lords of Calradia lived in a cage of ink and parchment..."*

**Veo3 Prompt:**
```
Overhead view of a crude medieval map drawn in scratchy black ink on rough
aged parchment. Small rigid wooden carvings of knights scattered across the
map, completely frozen and motionless. Heavy dust settling on figures. Camera
slowly dollies forward. Dramatic flickering candlelight casts long imposing
shadows from off-screen. Dust motes drift through warm light. Nothing moves
except light and shadow. 2.5D parallax depth between map and figures.
Sepia and charcoal tones. Gothic historical art style.
```

**TRANSITION:** Slow dissolve to close-up.

### SCENE 2 — The Puppet
**Duration:** 0:18 – 0:32 (14s)
**Soundtrack cue:** Drone continues. Sparse lyre pluck enters — one note, echoing.

**VISUAL:**
Extreme close-up of a static ink sketch of a generic Bannerlord lord. Cross-hatched, lifeless, his mouth drawn shut. Behind him — bleeding through the parchment like iron-gall ghosts — mathematical UI nodes, dialogue tree lines (`[Option 1]`, `[Option 2]`), and rigid geometric gears. The mechanics that trap him are visible through the page. He cannot speak. He cannot choose. He is wood.

**CAMERA:** Locked static shot. Only the candle flickers. The ghost-UI elements pulse faintly underneath.

**NARRATION (V.O.):**
*"Locked in cycles of words already written. Men of wood. Kings of clockwork."*

**Veo3 Prompt:**
```
Extreme close-up 2.5D parallax illustration of a stoic emotionless medieval
lord drawn in highly detailed cross-hatched black ink on aged parchment. His
mouth is drawn shut, face completely frozen. Behind him, faint geometric
lines, mechanical gears, and dialogue tree text bleed through the parchment
like ghostly watermarks. Heavy shadows from flickering candle. The drawing
is beautiful but lifeless. Gothic manuscript style, sepia charcoal tones.
```

**TRANSITION:** Hold on the frozen face. Then —

### SCENE 3 — The Drop
**Duration:** 0:32 – 0:38 (6s)
**Soundtrack cue:** **CUT to #5 Trailer** — sub-bass rumble begins. The drone from Ambient dies. A deep, tectonic vibration enters.

**VISUAL:**
A single drop of dark, glowing ethereal blue liquid falls from above — the ONLY blue light in the entire trailer. It strikes the center of the parchment. The ink boils. The parchment cracks and burns outward from the impact point. The old drawings fracture and curl.

**CAMERA:** Tight overhead. The drop falls in slow motion. Impact is sudden and violent.

**AUDIO:** Deep resonant bass impact. The sound of cracking wood and tearing parchment.

**NARRATION (V.O.):**
*"But the old bindings have broken."*

**Veo3 Prompt:**
```
Overhead view of aged parchment with ink drawings. A single drop of
glowing ethereal blue liquid fire falls from above in slow motion, striking
the center of the page. On impact, the ink violently boils and fractures,
the parchment burns outward in cracks and curling edges. High contrast,
visceral energy. The blue glow is the only color — everything else is
sepia and charcoal. 2.5D parallax ink wash style. Embers rise from the
burning point.
```

**TRANSITION:** Hard cut to black. Beat of silence (0.5s). Then —

## ACT II — THE AWAKENING

*Soundtrack: #5 Trailer — building from heartbeat drum to full escalation*

---

### SCENE 4 — Eyes Open
**Duration:** 0:39 – 0:50 (11s)
**Soundtrack cue:** Single heartbeat drum. Sub-bass drone solidifying. Bowed horsehair drone enters, scratchy.

**VISUAL:**
The same ink lord from Scene 2 — but his eyes MOVE. They dart up. They look directly at the camera. The UI dialogue tree lines behind him ignite and burn away into ash. The sketch rapidly evolves: crude medieval drawing → highly detailed, emotionally expressive ink art. His face gains depth, shadow, pain, intelligence. He is no longer wood. He is alive.

**CAMERA:** Same locked close-up as Scene 2. But the drawing CHANGES — the transformation happens within the frame.

**NARRATION (V.O.):**
*"The breath of a new mind has swept across the continent."*

**Veo3 Prompt:**
```
Extreme close-up of a medieval ink illustration of a lord on aged parchment.
His frozen eyes suddenly dart upward and look directly at the camera. Behind
him, geometric UI lines and gears violently ignite and burn away into ash
and embers. The crude cross-hatched drawing rapidly transforms, gaining
incredible detail — shadows deepen, expression emerges, the face becomes
alive and emotionally complex. The transformation from puppet to person.
2.5D parallax, flickering candlelight, sepia and charcoal with embers.
```

**TRANSITION:** Quick dissolve to montage.

### SCENE 6 — The Brothers
**Duration:** 1:15 – 1:35 (20s)
**Soundtrack cue:** Main Theme kicks in. Deep horn. Frame drums pounding. Hurdy-gurdy grinding underneath. Monumental.

**VISUAL:**
Wide parallax scene: the three Lothbrok brothers — Raghnar, Erik, Ubbe — ride on horseback across a frozen Nord landscape. Barren trees, iron-gray sky, snow on the ground. The ink is detailed and alive — cloaks billow, horses breathe steam, weapons glint. Behind them, a column of warriors follows. The warband is small but moving with purpose.

The scene has LIFE. Wind moves the cloaks. Breath rises from the horses. This is the opposite of Scene 1's frozen map.

**CAMERA:** Wide tracking shot, left to right. Multiple parallax layers: foreground snow, mid-ground riders, background mountains and sky.

**NARRATION (V.O.):**
*"In the north, three brothers rode out from the ruins of their father's hall. Three swords. One dream."*

**Veo3 Prompt:**
```
Wide 2.5D parallax scene of three Norse warriors on horseback riding across
a frozen winter landscape. Barren trees, iron-gray sky, snow on the ground.
Detailed ink illustration with life and movement — cloaks billowing, horses
breathing steam, weapons visible. Behind them follows a small column of
warriors. Multiple parallax layers creating depth: foreground snow, mid-ground
riders, distant mountains. Cross-hatched gothic manuscript style but ALIVE
and dynamic. Sepia and charcoal with touches of dried-blood red on cloaks
and banners. Flickering warm light from an unseen low sun.
```

This is the AI. The mind of the world.

**CAMERA:** Wide pull-back revealing the full hall. Then slow push-in toward the monolith. The blue light intensifies.

## Production Notes

### Art Style Reference
- **Primary:** Bannerlord campaign intro (2.5D parallax ink illustrations)
- **Secondary:** Darkest Dungeon narrator aesthetic, Disco Elysium thought cabinet
- **Ink quality:** Iron-gall ink on parchment — scratchy, imperfect, human
- **Never:** Clean digital art, anime, photorealism (except Scene 9 transition)

### Narration Direction
- Voice: Male, 40s-50s, deep, weathered, slightly exhausted
- Delivery: Slow, deliberate, like a man telling a story by a dying fire
- NOT: Dramatic movie trailer voice. NOT energetic. NOT epic announcer.
- Reference: Darkest Dungeon narrator tone, but warmer and more tired

### Key Constraint
The trailer must work WITHOUT gameplay footage. Everything is illustrated in the manuscript style. The only "modern" element is the Monolith's blue glow — and that contrast IS the point. Ancient world. Alien mind.
