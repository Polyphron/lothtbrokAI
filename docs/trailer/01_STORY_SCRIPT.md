# LothbrokAI: Cinematic Teaser Script

## Tone & Pacing
Slow, atmospheric, heavy. Driven by a deep, gritty, slightly exhausted voice. The aesthetic mirrors the vanilla Bannerlord intro: 2.5D parallax illustrations drawn with scratchy ink on worn parchment, illuminated by flickering candlelight.

---

### SCENE 1
**VISUAL:** The ink lines of the vanilla Calradic map quickly sketch themselves onto parchment. But the map feels rigid, frozen. Small wooden carved figures of lords are scattered across the map, completely motionless, locked in static positions. Dust settles heavily on them. 
**AUDIO:** (Silence, save for the scratching of a quill and a cold winter wind)
**NARRATOR (V.O.):** 
"For an age, the Lords of Calradia lived in a cage of ink and parchment..."

### SCENE 2
**VISUAL:** Close-up of a static, unmoving ink sketch of a generic Bannerlord lord. His mouth is drawn shut. Mathematical UI nodes and dialogue tree lines (`[Option 1]`, `[Option 2]`) faintly bleed through the parchment like iron-gall ghosts, showing the rigid mechanics trapping him.
**NARRATOR (V.O.):** 
"Locked in cycles of words already written. Men of wood. Kings of clockwork."

### SCENE 3
**VISUAL:** A drop of dark, glowing blue liquid (or liquid fire) falls from above, striking the center of the parchment. It burns its way through. 
**AUDIO:** (A deep, resonant bass drop. The sound of cracking wood.)
**NARRATOR (V.O.):** 
"But the old bindings have broken."

### SCENE 4
**VISUAL:** The ink illustration of the Lord *moves*. His eyes dart up. The UI dialogue lines burn away violently into ash. He turns to look directly at the camera. The sketch rapidly evolves from a crude medieval drawing into highly detailed, emotionally expressive ink art.
**NARRATOR (V.O.):** 
"The breath of a new mind has swept across the continent."

### SCENE 5
**VISUAL:** Montage of 2.5D parallax ink scenes, accelerating in speed:
1. Two shadowy lords whispering in an alleyway, passing gold coins (Bribery/Manipulation).
2. A fierce shieldmaiden roaring, eyes wide with genuine fury, severing an alliance banner with an axe (Dynamic War Cascades).
3. A king sitting alone by a fire, his head in his hands, surrounded by floating phantom memories of past betrayals (The Medici Memory Engine).
**AUDIO:** (Swelling, thumping war drums overlayed with the chaotic whispers of a thousand overlapping voices).
**NARRATOR (V.O.):** 
"They no longer just speak. They plot. They scheme. They remember. They *feel*."

### SCENE 6
**VISUAL:** The camera pans out to reveal the grand mead hall from the main banner. The illustration transitions from scratching ink into full, breathtaking color and volumetric lighting. The Viking warlord stands before the glowing UI monolith, the true mind of the world. 
**NARRATOR (V.O.):** 
"Calradia is awake."

### TITLE SCENE
**VISUAL:** The glowing monolithic sphere flares brilliantly, blinding the screen, burning out into the dark iron logo.
**LOTHBROK AI**
*A Living Intelligence Mod for Bannerlord*

**NARRATOR (V.O.):** 
"Choose your words carefully... for they will not forget."
